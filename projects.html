My Projects

Tax Calculator Website

I worked as an intern at The Money Concern, a startup, where I created a React JS tax calculator website. Taking into account factors like location, base pay, dearness allowance, HRA, LTA, and rent paid, this platform simplifies the process of calculating income. Even though my internship ended after a month, the financial company intends to improve the website for thorough tax computation.

The Blue Ball Game, which I created using Unity and C#, is my first game development program project. My journey into game production began with this practical experience.

Dietricxs Application

This is an Android application made with the help of Android studio, with the language Java. This is the application that I made with my friend in my college as a project. In this app, You can tell what you ate, and then it will tell you how much vitamins, proteins, calories,etc. you gained as well as other essential details about your total diet. It has a feaature of weekly history and login to save your progress. It uses Google firebase as the database to save all your login information and your dietary information.

I co-developed the Android app Dietricxs as part of a semester 4 collegiate design project. It stores weekly food history and user credentials and is integrated with Google Firebase Database. Once users enter meal specifics, the app calculates various nutrients like vitamins, protein, and calories. Direct contact with dietitians for individualized advice is the goal of further iterations.

Lunar Odyssey Game

The Lunar Odyssey Game is a team project that was created at a hackathon. It honors the accomplishment of India's Chandrayan 3 mission and was created in Unity using C#. Controlling Chandrayan 3, players must navigate lunar obstacles to get to water sources. I worked on the backend development of the game while my friend concentrated on level design, demonstrating how well we worked together.
